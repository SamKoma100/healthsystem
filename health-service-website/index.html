Emergency Support

If someone is unconscious, bleeding heavily, struggling to breathe, or having severe chest pain, seek emergency help immediately.

  • Call the nearest hospital or local emergency contact.
  • Keep the patient calm and do not give unknown medicine.
  • For fever with confusion, severe dehydration, or pregnancy bleeding, go to a health facility urgently.
